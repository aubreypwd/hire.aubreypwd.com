Why Performance?

Performance matters because the systems that power the web don't care about your message or products—ouch! They only care about how well your site performs inside that system. If your site is slow or inefficient, those systems stop promoting it, and the people who do reach it leave within seconds.

Real world data shows that even small delays reduce conversions, increase abandonment, and hurt rankings. A performant site doesn't just fast when it loads—it behaves efficiently within these systems before they even get there, which is why performant experiences earn more trust, generate more leads, and produce better results.

That's why I build performance-first. Performance isn't something I do at the end; it guides every technical decision from the very beginning—no matter the project.
